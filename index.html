For Two Companions

Sarah & Peter.
Time-Lord-fluent.

For Sarah, who can name every monster in Genesis of the Daleksbefore the title card finishes — and for Peter, who watched it the first time around when the BBC had a one-camera budget and a wobbly set.

This is your archive. Every Doctor, every companion, every monster indexed below is here because the two of you wanted to know — across two generations, one family, sixty-three years of wandering.

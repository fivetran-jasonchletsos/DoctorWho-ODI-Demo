Doctor 1

An Adventure in Space and Time

83 min

Canon-Anchor

BBC docudrama about the making of Doctor Who, 1963. David Bradley as William Hartnell.

Mark Gatiss's love letter to the show's origin: Verity Lambert is the BBC's first woman producer, Waris Hussein directs from a shooting script written on a fortnight's deadline, and William Hartnell — sick, scared of being forgotten — is the first Doctor. Co-canonised when Bradley returned as the First Doctor proper in Twice Upon a Time.

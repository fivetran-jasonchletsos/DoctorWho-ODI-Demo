Cross-Canon

Connections

The deep cuts. The things you point out at the pub. Twelve of the franchise's confirmed-but-easy-to-miss cross-canon links.

  1. Link 01

    Captain Jack Harkness ↔ The Face of Boe

    Confirmed by Russell T Davies. Jack ages so slowly across billions of years his face becomes the giant glass-jar entity Ten meets twice. "You are not alone."

  2. Link 02

    Sutekh (1975) ↔ Empire of Death (2024)

    Sutekh has been riding the outside of the TARDIS, undetected, for forty-nine real-world years. The Doctor wins by dragging him through the time vortex on a rope.

  3. Link 03

    Adric's death (1982) ↔ Yucatán impact (65 million BC)

    Earthshock's Cyber-freighter slingshots back through time and becomes the asteroid that killed the dinosaurs. Established in the same serial; rarely brought up.

  4. Link 04

    Amy & Rory ↔ River Song

    Conceived in the TARDIS in flight; the time-vortex exposure made Melody Pond a Time Lord at the genetic level. Raised by the Silence to assassinate the Doctor. She marries him instead.

  5. Link 05

    The Brigadier ↔ Kate Stewart

    Father and daughter. Kate took over UNIT; the Brigadier saluted Twelve as a Cyberman in Death in Heaven. Five Doctors of continuity, one family.

  6. Link 06

    Susan Foreman ↔ The Doctor

    Her granddaughter. The show's oldest unresolved thread — Susan called him "Grandfather" in 1963 and the franchise has never given a straight answer. Possibly resolved by the Timeless Child reveal; possibly not.

  7. Link 07

    The Doctor ↔ The Timeless Child

    Thirteen learns she is the Timeless Child — a found alien orphan whose regeneration ability was harvested by the Time Lords. Every regeneration in Gallifreyan history descends from her, the original.

  8. Link 08

    Donna Noble ↔ Rose Noble (her daughter)

    The DoctorDonna metacrisis didn't burn Donna up — it became hereditary. Rose Noble inherited it; in The Star Beast she rejects the male-coded "Doctor Donna" name and binds it.

  9. Link 09

    Sarah Jane Smith ↔ Coal Hill School

    The school Ian and Barbara taught at in 1963 is the same school Clara teaches at in 2014 (and Yasmin's sister attends in 2018). Sarah Jane Adventures, Class, and main-series all converge there.

  10. Link 10

    The Master ↔ Missy

    Same person. The Master regenerated into Missy (Michelle Gomez) and back into Dhawan's Master, who killed his own previous self in Doctor Falls. The franchise's only confirmed Time Lord trans arc.

  11. Link 11

    Charlotte Pollard ↔ The Eight Doctor / The Night of the Doctor

    Big Finish audio companion. Eight name-checks her in the Night of the Doctor minisode ("Charley. C'rizz. Lucie…"), making decades of audio canon officially TV-canonical.

  12. Link 12

    The Fugitive Doctor ↔ Ruth Clayton

    Pre-Hartnell incarnation hiding in 21st-century Gloucester as a tour guide with a chameleon arch. The most consequential canon-breaker since the Time War.

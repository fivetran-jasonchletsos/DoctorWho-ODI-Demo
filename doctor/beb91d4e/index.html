Incarnation 11

Matt Smith

2010–2013

Costume

Tweed jacket, bow tie, fez ("fezes are cool"), Stetson

Defining Moment

The Pandorica Opens / The Big Bang — "I am the Doctor."

Old man in a young body. Cracks in the universe, the Silence, River Song's whole life lived backwards. Lived 900 years on Trenzalore; died of old age and started over.
